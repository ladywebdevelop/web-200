Tom Edwards, is a prominent individual in the business and community. He was born Februray 8, 1957, to Rosa and Don. He mentors, donates both money and time, and ensures that his employees are well taken care of. He went to Doniphan High School graduating in 1975.

In his spare time he gardens, takes care of his family, enjoys his dogs and cats, classic cars and socializing.

In Grand Island, Nebraska you will not find another place that has won as many awards as his restaurant, Tommy's, for Best Broasted Chicken.
